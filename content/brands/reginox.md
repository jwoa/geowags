Reginox is a European leader in stainless steel sink manufacturing, offering a comprehensive range of kitchen sinks and accessories. Known for their durability and elegant designs, Reginox sinks are crafted from premium-grade stainless steel.

Their product range includes single and double bowl sinks, undermount options, and complete sink packages with matching taps and accessories, all designed to meet the demands of modern kitchens.
