## Product Care & Maintenance

### How do I clean and maintain tile floors?
- Regular sweeping or vacuuming to remove dirt
- Damp mopping with pH-neutral cleaners
- Avoid harsh chemicals that can damage grout or finishes
- Seal grout lines annually for best results
- Address spills promptly to prevent staining

### What maintenance do bathroom fixtures require?
- Clean regularly with mild soap and water
- Avoid abrasive cleaners that can scratch finishes
- Wipe dry after use to prevent water spots
- Check and clean aerators periodically
- Inspect for leaks and address promptly

### Do ceramic and porcelain tiles need sealing?
Glazed ceramic and porcelain tiles generally don't require sealing. Natural stone tiles (marble, granite, slate) and cement tiles should be sealed to protect against stains and moisture. We provide sealing recommendations with each product.
