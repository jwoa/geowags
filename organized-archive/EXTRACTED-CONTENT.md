## About Us Content (Verified from Archive)

### Company History

**Founding (1981):**
Geowags was established in 1981 by Mr. George Wiafe-Agyekum as a sole proprietorship with a mission to make quality sanitary ware, tiles, paints, and kitchen products available to the Ghanaian market.

**Incorporation (1990):**
The company was incorporated as a limited liability company in 1990, marking an expansion of operations and the establishment of a network of showrooms and affiliates across Ghana.

**Brand Philosophy:**
> "The modern bathroom has moved from being purely functional to a room in which you can express yourself... a dynamic hub in the morning for the family to invigorate themselves in for the day ahead... a sanctuary in the evening for relaxation and re-charging the batteries!"
