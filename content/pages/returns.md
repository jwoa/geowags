## Returns Overview

At Geowags, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we offer a fair and transparent returns process.

## Return Eligibility

### Items That Can Be Returned

You may return items that meet ALL of the following criteria:
- Purchased within the last 14 days
- In original, unused condition
- In original packaging with all labels intact
- Not installed or altered in any way
- Include all accessories, manuals, and components
- Have proof of purchase (receipt or order confirmation)

### Items That Cannot Be Returned

The following items are **not eligible for return**:
- Custom-ordered or specially manufactured items
- Cut tiles or modified products
- Installed or used products
- Items without original packaging
- Products showing signs of installation attempts
- Special orders made to customer specifications
- Clearance or final sale items
- Products damaged due to mishandling or misuse

## Return Reasons

### Acceptable Return Reasons
- Changed mind (within 14 days, unused)
- Ordered wrong product
- No longer needed
- Color/style doesn't match expectations

### Quality Issues
- Manufacturing defects
- Shipping damage
- Incorrect items shipped
- Missing components

**Note**: Quality issues are handled differently and may be covered by warranty. See Warranty section below.

## Return Process

### Step 1: Contact Us
Before returning any items:
- Call us at +233 XX XXX XXXX or email info@geowags.com
- Provide your order number
- Explain reason for return
- Include photos if applicable (for damaged items)

### Step 2: Receive Return Authorization
- We'll review your request
- Issue a Return Authorization (RA) number if approved
- Provide return shipping instructions
- Confirm eligible refund amount

**Important**: Do not ship items without an RA number. Unauthorized returns may not be accepted.

### Step 3: Prepare Items for Return
- Keep items in original packaging
- Include all accessories and documentation
- Write RA number clearly on outside of package
- Take photos of items and packaging before shipping
- Ensure items are securely packaged

### Step 4: Ship Items Back
- Use a trackable shipping method
- Insure valuable shipments
- Keep shipping receipt and tracking number
- Customer pays return shipping costs (unless item is defective)

**Shipping Address** (provided with RA number):  
Geowags Returns Department  
Accra, Ghana

### Step 5: Processing
- We inspect items within 2-3 business days of receipt
- Verify items meet return criteria
- Process approved refunds within 7-14 business days
- Notify you of inspection results

## Return Shipping Costs

### Standard Returns
- Customer pays return shipping
- We recommend insured, trackable shipping
- Save your shipping receipt

### Defective or Damaged Items
- We cover return shipping costs
- We'll provide a prepaid shipping label or arrange pickup
- Follow provided instructions for return

### Exchanges
- We cover shipping both ways for exchanges of defective items
- Customer pays shipping for exchange due to preference change

## Refunds

### Refund Amount
**Full Refund** (items returned in perfect condition):
- Product price refunded in full
- Original shipping charges are non-refundable
- Refund issued to original payment method

**Partial Refund** (items with minor issues):
- Determined on case-by-case basis
- Typically 10-30% reduction
- Depends on nature and extent of issue

**No Refund**:
- Items that don't meet return criteria
- Items damaged after delivery
- Installed or altered products
- Items without proper return authorization

### Restocking Fee
A 15% restocking fee may apply to:
- Returns due to buyer's remorse
- Wrong item ordered by customer
- No longer needed items
- Non-defective bulk orders

**Waived For**:
- Defective products
- Our shipping errors
- Damaged items
- Quality issues

### Refund Timeline
- Inspection: 2-3 business days after receipt
- Refund processing: 3-5 business days
- Bank processing: 5-10 business days
- Total time: 10-18 business days

### Refund Method
- Issued to original payment method
- Bank transfer refunds: 5-7 business days
- Mobile money: 1-2 business days
- Cash on delivery orders: Bank transfer to your account

## Exchanges

### Exchange Process
We offer exchanges for:
- Different size of the same product
- Different color of the same product
- Different product of equal or greater value

**How to Exchange**:
1. Follow standard return process
2. Indicate desired exchange item
3. We'll ship exchange item upon receiving return
4. Pay price difference if applicable

### Exchange Shipping
- Free shipping for defective item exchanges
- Customer pays shipping difference for preference exchanges
- New item ships after return is received and inspected

## Damaged or Defective Items

### Immediate Action Required
If you receive damaged or defective items:
1. **Do not refuse delivery** - Accept and note damage on receipt
2. **Document immediately** - Take clear photos of:
   - Damaged packaging
   - Damaged products
   - All sides/angles
   - Labels and markings
3. **Report within 48 hours** - Contact us immediately
4. **Keep everything** - Don't discard packaging or materials
5. **Don't install** - Do not attempt installation

### Our Response
For damaged/defective items:
- We'll arrange pickup at our expense
- Issue immediate replacement or refund
- Expedited processing (2-3 business days)
- No restocking fee
- No cost to you

## Warranty Claims

### Manufacturer Warranties
Many products come with manufacturer warranties:
- Coverage period varies (1-5 years typical)
- Covers manufacturing defects
- Does not cover installation damage or wear and tear
- Requires proof of purchase

### Warranty Process
1. Contact us with product details and issue description
2. Provide photos and purchase documentation
3. We'll facilitate warranty claim with manufacturer
4. Manufacturer determines coverage
5. Repair, replacement, or refund per manufacturer policy

### Our Support
We help with:
- Warranty claim initiation
- Communication with manufacturer
- Documentation preparation
- Claim follow-up

## Special Circumstances

### Bulk Orders
Returns of bulk orders:
- Must be unused and in original packaging
- May incur 20% restocking fee
- Partial returns accepted
- Contact us for special arrangements

### Commercial Projects
For commercial project returns:
- Different terms may apply
- Discuss with your account manager
- Project-specific return agreements possible

### Force Majeure
Returns may be affected by:
- Natural disasters
- Political instability
- Pandemic restrictions
- Other circumstances beyond our control

We'll work with you to find reasonable solutions.

### Partial Cancellation
