Vado is a British manufacturer renowned for producing high-quality bathroom and kitchen brassware. From elegant basin taps to sophisticated shower systems, Vado combines British design heritage with modern manufacturing excellence.

Their extensive range includes mixer taps, shower valves, and accessories, all crafted with precision engineering and available in a variety of contemporary finishes including chrome, brushed nickel, and matte black.
