## Shipping Coverage

We provide delivery services throughout Ghana, ensuring that quality home improvement products reach every corner of the country.

### Delivery Zones

**Zone 1: Greater Accra Region**
- Accra Metropolitan Area
- Tema
- Kasoa
- Surrounding communities
- Delivery time: 2-5 business days

**Zone 2: Major Cities**
- Kumasi
- Takoradi
- Cape Coast
- Tamale
- Other regional capitals
- Delivery time: 5-7 business days

**Zone 3: All Other Areas**
- Towns and rural communities
- Remote locations
- Delivery time: 7-10 business days

## Shipping Costs

### Standard Delivery

**Zone 1 (Greater Accra)**
- Orders under GHS 2,000: GHS 100
- Orders GHS 2,000 - GHS 5,000: GHS 150
- Orders over GHS 5,000: FREE

**Zone 2 (Major Cities)**
- Orders under GHS 2,000: GHS 200
- Orders GHS 2,000 - GHS 5,000: GHS 250
- Orders over GHS 5,000: GHS 100

**Zone 3 (Other Areas)**
- Shipping costs calculated based on:
  - Distance
  - Order weight and volume
  - Accessibility
  - Contact us for a quote

### Special Delivery Services

**Express Delivery (Accra Only)**
- Next business day delivery
- Additional cost: GHS 200
- Order must be placed before 2:00 PM
- Subject to availability

**White Glove Delivery**
- Inside delivery and placement
- Debris removal
- Professional handling
- Additional cost varies by item
- Available in Greater Accra

## Order Processing

### Processing Times
- In-stock items: 1-2 business days
- Special orders: 3-4 weeks
- Custom items: 4-6 weeks

### What Happens After You Order

1. **Order Confirmation**: You'll receive an email confirming your order
2. **Processing**: We prepare your items for shipment
3. **Quality Check**: All products are inspected before shipping
4. **Dispatch Notification**: You'll be notified when items ship
5. **Delivery Coordination**: Our team will contact you to schedule delivery
6. **Delivery**: Items arrive at your specified location

## Delivery Process

### Before Delivery
- We'll contact you 24-48 hours before delivery
- Confirm delivery address and access details
- Schedule a convenient delivery window
- Provide contact information for the day of delivery

### On Delivery Day
- Our delivery team will call 1 hour before arrival
- Delivery window: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM
- Someone must be present to receive the delivery
- Photo ID may be required

### What to Expect
- Delivery to ground floor or curbside (standard)
- Basic visual inspection possible
- Sign delivery receipt upon satisfaction
- Report any visible damage immediately

## Packaging and Handling

### Our Standards
- Professional packaging for all products
- Protective materials for fragile items
- Secure palletizing for bulk orders
- Weather-resistant wrapping

### Heavy or Oversized Items
Items including:
- Large format tiles (palletized)
- Bathtubs and shower enclosures
- Large bathroom vanities
- Bulk orders

May require:
- Multiple delivery personnel
- Special equipment (forklift, pallet jack)
- Ground level delivery only
- Advance scheduling

## Delivery Requirements

### Access Requirements
Ensure you have:
- Clear path to delivery location
- Adequate doorway width (minimum 800mm)
- Working elevator (for high-rise deliveries)
- Parking available for delivery vehicle
- No stairs (or coordinate special handling)

### Receiving Inspection
Upon delivery:
1. Verify quantity against delivery receipt
2. Inspect packaging for obvious damage
3. Check product condition (open boxes if needed)
4. Note any concerns on delivery receipt
5. Take photos of any damage
6. Sign only if satisfied

### Damage Reporting
- Report visible damage immediately during delivery
- Contact us within 48 hours for concealed damage
- Provide clear photos of damage
- Keep all packaging materials
- Do not install damaged products

## Special Circumstances

### Delivery Delays
Deliveries may be delayed due to:
- Extreme weather conditions
- Road closures or traffic
- Inaccessible delivery locations
- Incomplete delivery information
- Force majeure events

We'll notify you of any significant delays.

### Failed Delivery Attempts
If delivery cannot be completed:
- We'll attempt to contact you
- Reschedule for next available date
- Storage fees may apply after 48 hours
- Orders may be returned after multiple failed attempts

### Refused Deliveries
- Return shipping charges apply
- Restocking fee: 15% of order value
- Refund processed within 7-14 days
- Custom items cannot be refused

## Installation Considerations

### Important Notes
- We deliver products only; installation not included
- Ensure you have a qualified installer arranged
- Review installation requirements before delivery
- Coordinate delivery timing with your installer
- Some products require special installation equipment

### Installer Referrals
We work with trusted contractors and can provide referrals for:
- Tile installation
- Bathroom fixture installation
- Plumbing services
- Electrical work (for fixtures with electrical components)
